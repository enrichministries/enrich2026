About The Event

Elevate Your Worship

Enrich 2026 is not just a workshop; it is a spiritual convocation designed to equip the next generation of Levites. Whether you are holding a guitar for the first time or leading a congregation of thousands, this gathering is crafted to refine your skill and renew your spirit.

Mission of National Youth Ministries: We exist to reach, teach, and unleash young people into their God-given destiny. Through Enrich 2026, we aim to build a strong foundation of biblical worship across our churches.

Soli Deo Gloria

Workshop Syllabus

Comprehensive tracks designed to sharpen your skills and deepen your understanding of worship.

Music Track

Guitar

From basics to advanced techniques. Focus on chord voicing, fingerstyle, and lead lines specifically for modern worship songs.

Keyboard & Piano

Mastering chord progressions, padding, layering sounds, and the art of prophetic improvisation.

Drums & Percussion

Understanding dynamics, groove, and rhythmic patterns that serve the song without overpowering it.

Vocals

Breath control, range expansion, harmonization, and the heart of a vocal minister.

Media & Tech

Sound mixing, livestreaming essentials, and creating visual presentations that enhance the worship atmosphere.

Core Curriculum: Theory & Musicianship

Fundamentals & Scales

Introduction, Formation of Major Scale, Understanding Minor Scales.

Chords & Harmony

Triads (Major, Minor, Aug, Dim), Sevenths, Suspended, Complex Chord Formations.

Chord Progressions

Roman Numeral Analysis Natural Formation, Analysis (I-IV-V, I-vi-IV-V7, I-ii-IV-V, etc.), Technical Terms.

Rhythm & Application

Time Signatures (2/4, 4/4, 3/4, 6/8), understanding groove and Chord Colors.

Playing Worship Songs

Working as a unified band, developing Musicianship, and effective Practice habits.

Worship Track

Worship Vision

Defining the core purpose, biblical foundation, and prophetic direction of why we gather to worship.

Lifestyle of a Worshiper

Cultivating intimacy with God that extends beyond the stage, ensuring public ministry flows from private devotion.

Engaging the Realm of the Spirit

Learning to sense the Holy Spirit's movement, discern the atmosphere, and flow with His direction during service.

What does a worship leader do?

Defining the multifaceted role: a shepherd to the team, a priest before God, and a musical director for the congregation.

Spirit, Word and Worship

Balancing theological depth with spiritual vitality to ensure our worship is both truthful and alive.

Building Local Church Ministry

Practical strategies for recruitment, training, discipleship, and sustaining a healthy team culture.

Worship and Warfare

Understanding praise as a spiritual weapon to break strongholds, shift atmospheres, and declare victory.

How to choose worship songs?

The art of curating setlists that serve the congregation, fit the season, and align with the sermon theme.

What happens when we play music

Exploring the spiritual, physical, and emotional impact of anointed sound and the theology of music.

Emerging Paradigms

Navigating modern trends, technology, and future directions in the global ministry of worship leading.
